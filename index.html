Privacy Policy How we handle your data

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when registering, including your name, email address, class, section, and house. We also collect attendance records, event participation, and academic results.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage student profiles and academic records
  • To track event registrations and attendance
  • To generate certificates of participation and achievement
  • To communicate important announcements

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (PostgreSQL) servers with row-level security policies in place. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

4. Cookies & Sessions

We use session tokens to keep you logged in. These are stored in your browser's local storage and are cleared on logout.

5. Your Rights

You may request to view, update, or delete your personal data by contacting your school administrator.

6. Contact

For privacy-related queries, contact the school administration at Kasidih High School, Jamshedpur.

Terms & Conditions Rules for using KHS Activity Hub

Last updated: June 2026

1. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing KHS Activity Hub, you agree to these terms. If you do not agree, please do not use the platform.

2. Acceptable Use

  • Use the platform only for legitimate school activities
  • Do not share your login credentials with others
  • Do not attempt to access other users' data
  • Report any bugs or security issues to the admin

3. Student Accounts

Student accounts are created upon registration. Your account is tied to your admission at Kasidih High School. Accounts may be deactivated upon leaving the school.

4. Event Registration

By registering for events, you agree to participate in good faith and abide by event rules set by the school.

5. Content & Certificates

Certificates generated through this platform are official school documents. Misuse or forgery of certificates is a disciplinary offence.

6. Changes to Terms

The school reserves the right to update these terms at any time. Continued use of the platform implies acceptance of updated terms.
